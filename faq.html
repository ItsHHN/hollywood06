1. What is Halloween Horror Nights?
Universal Studios Hollywood is transformed into Los Angeles’s most terrifying Halloween experience, filled with haunted walk-through attractions, roaming scare-actors, and nightmare-inducing rides, shows and attractions. Plus unforgettable live music and high-energy dance performances by “The Mutaytor.”

Halloween Horror Nights is a separately ticketed nighttime event that is not included with regular daytime admission.

Halloween Horror Nights takes place on October 13–14, 20–21,
27–28 & 31.

2. Will the regular rides and attractions be open?
Universal Studios Hollywood's favorite attractions will remain open for the nighttime Halloween Horror Nights experience, but many will feature a menacing twist.

The standing sets and themed streets of the park will be transformed into scenes from "The Director's" dark vision. "WaterWorld" (transformed into "Slaughter World" for the occasion), "Jurassic Park—In the Dark," the "Revenge of the Mummy" psychological thrill ride and the twists and turns of  "Back to the Future... The Ride" will all seem like tranquil respites from The Director's bedlam.

3. Is Halloween Horror Nights suitable for children?
This event may be too intense for young children. Some material may be inappropriate for children under 13. There is no minimum age requirement for admission; however, adult discretion is strongly advised. There are no child ticket prices for this event.

4. When (dates and hours) is Halloween Horror Nights?
The first night is Friday the 13th of October. 

Event nights: 
October 13, 14, 20, 21, 27, 28, and 31.

Event hours:
7PM–Midnight on the nights of October 13, 14, 20, 27, and 31.
7PM–2AM on the nights of October 21 and 28.

5. Are costumes allowed?
No. Costumes, make-up and masks are not allowed. Guests wearing costumes will be turned away at the front gate.

6. How do I purchase tickets?
You can buy your tickets online.

Tickets are also available for purchase at the Front Gate ticket booths, inside the park at the Hollywood Ticket Outlet and on Universal CityWalk at the Universal Studios Store.

7. What is the price of Halloween Horror Nights tickets?
General admission is $49.00 the day of the event.

Yes, quantities are limited, so it's recommended to purchase your tickets as early as possible.

9. Is Halloween Horror Nights admission free for Annual Pass holders?
No, it is not. Halloween Horror Nights is a specially ticketed event. However, there are specially-discounted Halloween Horror Nights tickets available for purchase by Annual Pass holders only.

To purchase your exclusive Annual Pass holder discount tickets, first enter your Annual Pass barcode (located on the back of your pass) on the Tickets page and then hit “Go”.

10. Are VIP Tours available?
Yes.  For $129—which includes your admission to Halloween Horror Nights—you will receive a private tour guide for the evening.

As part of the VIP Tour, you will benefit from Front of Line and Reserved Seating privileges. You will also get to spend some time in the VIP lounge and enjoy a private vehicle to and from the “Terror Tram” attraction. 

These tickets are very limited and available only at the VIP box office next to the ticket booths, or by calling in advance: 818-622-5120

11. Is Halloween Horror Nights included with a regular daytime admission ticket?
No.  Halloween Horror Nights is a specially ticketed event. 

12. Is regular daytime admission included in a Halloween Horror Nights ticket?
No, it is not.

However, when buying a Halloweeen Horror Nights ticket online, you can also buy a regular daytime ticket at the same time using the same shopping cart.  In the upper left corner of our online ticket store, you will see links to the daytime ticket sections of the store.

13. Is there a way to skip lines for the event attractions and shows?
Yes, you can maximize your experience at Halloween Horror Nights with a Front of Line Pass, which provides shorter wait times at participating attractions!

The Front of Line Pass is valid for unlimited use at rides and shows, but only valid for one-time use at Asylum, House of Horrors and Terror Tram.

Buy your Front of Line pass now.

14. Are tickets for Universal Studios Hollywood’s Halloween Horror Nights valid at Universal Orlando® in Florida?
No, tickets are only valid at Universal Studios Hollywood located in Los Angeles, California. Tickets are not valid at any Universal Orlando® theme park.

15. Where is Universal Studios Hollywood located?
We are conveniently located right off the 101 (Hollywood) Freeway, approximately ten minutes north of Downtown Los Angeles. Exit either Universal Studios Boulevard or Lankershim Blvd.

Our address is 100 Universal City Plaza, Universal City, CA 91608.

18. Is it true, that we can walk on the backlot?
Yes.  For the first time in studio history, guests will take a tram to the studio and walk among some of the scariest sets on the backlot.

19. I heard The Mutaytor is performing at the event?  True?
Yes.  One of the feature attractions will be multiple nightly performances by music/performance artists The Mutaytor, a multi-media experience that was formed at the legendary Burning Man Festival by members of Oingo Boingo and other musical and theatrical groups.  The Mutaytor’s blend of pyrotechnics, stunt performance, tribal dancers and heart-pounding music will add a spine-tingling and electrifying element to the Halloween experience.
